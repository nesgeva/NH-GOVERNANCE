## 9. HELD-MATERIAL AND PROVISIONAL-MATERIAL BOUNDARY VERIFICATION

The plan's mandatory boundary — *"Held or provisional material must
not silently influence ordinary memory, ranking, interpretation,
snapshots, or output"* — covers **two different things**, and v1_0
verified only the second. They are separated here. A held activation
**request** is not held **content**; each relevant form is verified
below against accepted or governing bytes, with its exact boundary
and its owner. **No missing Bundle 5 or other outside-bundle
machinery is designed here** — only what already exists is cited.

**(a) A held activation request (B12 / C-7GA).** Not content: a
trigger-request record carries **no material** — it records that a
scheduled window or a manual request arrived and awaits admission.
Its only possible effect is **run admission**, and it is durable and
discoverable after a crash rather than living in memory. It cannot
enter memory, ranking, interpretation, snapshots, or output because
it contains nothing to enter them with. **Boundary/owner:** B12,
accepted mechanical package §4.

**(b) Held pre-ingest material (§7E / C-7E).** The governing boundary
is Master V10's own, in the components that could otherwise be
influenced: **§7L (Person-Boxes)** — held pre-ingest raw content
*remains invisible to Person-Box semantic analysis* unless an
explicitly authorized inspection mode is later designed; only safe
source-carried metadata, lifecycle state, and blocker information may
be linked; and **§7M (Computed View)** — held pre-ingest raw content
*must not influence Computed View ranking, interpretation, or output*
unless such a mode is later designed; only safe metadata, lifecycle
state, and blocker information may be surfaced, and metadata-only
references never reveal or reconstruct the held content. Held
material is also **not in ordinary memory at all**: it is not a root
until promotion (§7E lifecycle `held → ready → promoting →
promoted`). This covers all five named surfaces — memory, ranking,
interpretation, snapshots, output. **Boundary/owner:** Master V10
§7E/§7L/§7M (governing, settled — **not Bundle 6's to design**);
every Bundle 6 root-producing path consumes it unchanged (AMP §3,
§11, §13).

**(c) Held reading material — "hold-until-enough" (A29 / B-HOLD).**
**Accepted**, with closure records: the A29 policy (`31d5a124`)
settles when holding rather than reading is right, and the B-HOLD
lifecycle (`406ab00f`) settles the mechanics. Its boundary, read from
the accepted file: a hold lives **entirely in external append-only
hold records referencing the item by identity** — *"A hold never
moves, copies, edits, re-stores, or re-writes the held item"* — and
its blocked-marking commit is *"the append-only enforcement record
consulted by every gate"*, so a held item is blocked at every
consuming gate rather than silently flowing; a held parent reading
*"blocks new use downstream by derivation."* **The protection stated
accurately:** holding **never forces a missing reading or
interpretation to be produced** (that is what holding is *instead*
of); **an item or reading that already exists may itself be placed
under a hold** — a hold is not a claim that no interpretation exists;
while held, the item or reading **gains no new ordinary downstream
use and cannot silently influence memory, ranking, interpretation,
snapshots, or output**, because the hold's append-only enforcement
record is consulted by **every applicable consuming gate**; a **held
parent blocks new downstream derivative use**; and **the underlying
item remains unchanged and preserved** (*"a hold never moves, copies,
edits, re-stores, or re-writes the held item"*). **Boundary/owner:**
B-HOLD + A29 (both accepted; **outside Bundle 6**, not redesigned
here); Bundle 6's nightly activation (B12) neither bypasses nor
weakens it.

**(d) Quarantined and review-waiting material.** Two forms, both
bounded: **(i) research material awaiting review (C-8 / B13)** —
structurally, no context in the isolated research environment has any
write path to `append_root()`, the Creation Store, readings, or any
production store; **the only exit is the review queue**, and
