## 4. THE BUNDLE 6 POLICY REGISTER

### A3 — Meaning-to-technical mapping (five questions)

**A3.1 — Grounded Keeper scope. Classification: (a) direct new Ness
decision (preserved answer: B).** "The Grounded Keeper" means **the
whole practical memory side of N.H**, not only Origin storage. It
includes the parts that preserve, carry, organize, connect, and
return material **without deciding what that material means**.
Meaning protection: the Keeper's scope is wide, but its DUMB nature
is unchanged — nothing in this scope decides meaning.

**A3.2 — Mixed-media Origin granularity. Classification: (a) direct
new Ness decision (preserved answer: A).** **Every real source item
remains its own Origin.** A voice recording, transcript, photograph,
message, document, or other source stays separate and unchanged — it
is never merged into another source. When several Origins belong to
the same real moment, event, or situation, N.H **connects them**
rather than merging them into one Origin. Later mechanical owner:
**B21** (schemas expressing separate connected Origins with explicit
relationships).

**A3.3 — Origin time fields. Classification: (a) direct new Ness
decision (preserved answer: the source item's reliable real original
date/time is its main real-life date).** N.H keeps different times
**separately**: when the source item was actually created, captured,
sent, or occurred; when N.H received or imported it; and when N.H
created its own record. The original source date is the normal main
date when it is reliably known. **Import time and record-creation
time never replace the original source time.** When the original
date cannot be reliably known or proven, **N.H leaves it unresolved
instead of guessing** — unknown source time remains honestly unknown.
Later mechanical owner: **B21** (the three time fields kept
separately; unknown remains unknown).

**A3.4 — "Those Three To Become A One" with no surrounding context.
Classification: (b) closed by existing governing rules (preserved
answer: already settled by the governing design).** The same reading
process still runs when no surrounding context is available. N.H
**records that no surroundings were available and why**, reads only
the target Origin alone, uses **only what the Origin itself
supports**, marks the result as **context-limited and revisable**,
and **never invents missing meaning**. Later context may produce a
**new** reading without replacing or rewriting the earlier one.
Nothing further to design at policy level; the reading machinery is
already governed.

**A3.5 — Holding inside a Person-Box. Classification: (b) closed by
existing governing rules (preserved answer: produced by the existing
Story Layer and Person-Box design).** Holding is **not** a separate
profile, summary, fact-box, second memory store, or replacement
memory. A person-related understanding is held through **linked,
separate objects**, including: original roots; readings; story
tellings with their perspective owner recorded; Ness-response
events; clashes; themes; and unresolved identity anchors and merge
proposals. **Ness's understanding remains visibly separate from the
original evidence and from other people's perspectives. It never
becomes a settled fact about the person merely because it is
repeated, recent, or strongly held.** The exact record and link
layout remains later mechanical design work — a mechanical
concretization, not a new meaning choice for Ness. Later mechanical
owner: Bundle 6 mechanical work where it touches Bundle 6 paths
(with the Story-Layer/Person-Box structures themselves governed by
their own existing design).

### A10 — Behavioral-observation authorization

**Classification: (b) closed by the governing default; no extra
signal-category exception is adopted for the current Bundle 6
target.** When Ness explicitly opens an authorized session with N.H,
N.H may record **all available authorized behavioral signals** inside
that session. It does **not** require separate permission for each
silence, interruption, timing pattern, voice measurement, or other
authorized signal within the authorized session. Meaning
protections, unchanged: this does **not** authorize capture outside
an authorized session, does **not** bypass privacy rules, and does
**not** create meaning labels from raw signals. **It is explicitly
recorded that no extra per-signal exception is adopted for the
current target;** any later wish to create a special exception for a
signal category would be a **new Ness decision**, not a current
Bundle 6 blocker.

### A12 — Immediate reaction to imported voice content

**Classification: (a) direct new Ness decision (connect it).** When
Ness listens to an imported voice item during an authorized session,
N.H may **link the immediate raw observation window during listening
and immediately afterward to that exact imported item**. N.H may
preserve **facts** such as timing, silence, interruption, stopping,
resuming, speaking immediately afterward, and permitted raw voice
measurements. **It must not turn those facts into an automatic
emotional, motivational, or meaning claim** — never "Ness was upset,"
"Ness agreed," or "the message caused relief"; no emotion, motive,
agreement, causality, or meaning is ever inferred from the raw
signals. The imported item remains its own Origin; reaction
observations remain their own roots or records; **they are connected,
not merged.** Later mechanical owners: the exact time-window
boundary, operation identity, duplicate prevention, crash recovery,
retry behavior, and relationship-link schema are mechanical design
work — **B-INT-3** (the imported-item reaction link through the
catalog wiring) with the Bundle 6 mechanical package.

### A13 — Provisional Creation detection and influence

**A13.1 — Detection policy. Classification: (a) direct new Ness
decision (capture as broadly as reasonably possible).** N.H should
create provisional Creation Records **even from small fragments,
partial thoughts, short phrases, unfinished sections, or pieces that
may later combine into a much larger idea**. The goal is to avoid
losing important creation merely because Ness thinks in sections.
**Broad capture does not mean automatic confirmation.**

**A13.2 — Influence policy. Classification: (a) direct new Ness
decision (both).** A provisional creation: (1) **remains available in
its own clear ideas-in-progress / provisional-creation area**; and
(2) **is allowed to influence wider retrieval, reasoning, answers,
and the Computed View when relevant.** Required status protections,
all preserved whole:

- Every wider use must **carry and preserve the record's provisional
  status**.
- N.H must **not** present a provisional fragment as a confirmed
  fact, adopted rule, final decision, or settled creation.
- **Time, repetition, retrieval frequency, system use, or repeated
  model output never confirms it.**
- Confirmation occurs **only** through the two settled routes: Ness's
  explicit confirmation, or Ness's later words/actions clearly
  demonstrating adoption.
- **Ambiguity remains provisional.**
- Use of provisional material must be **traceable in the operation
  record** so its influence is never silent.
- Possible relationships among fragments may be recorded or proposed,
  but the original fragments and their provenance remain traceable
  and are **not silently fused** into an adopted creation.

Later mechanical owner: **B14** (Creation Store schema and
lifecycle implementing exactly these protections).

**A13.3 — Later interface note. Classification: (d) future
non-blocking note — not current Bundle 6 policy work.** Ness wants
N.H to sometimes talk with him about a promising provisional
fragment so a small piece can grow into a larger idea. This is
preserved as a **future interface/conversation behavior note**. It
must not be silently designed inside Bundle 6, must not block Bundle
6, and is routed to the appropriate later interface work, including
**Bundle 7 / A19** where relevant.

### A33 — Live-conversation provenance-label meaning and scope

**Classification: (b) closed by the governing design; only format
mechanics remain.** The label means **only** that the material came
from a recorded live conversation between Ness and N.H. It must
preserve that: there are **two known speakers**; **Ness's words are
Ness's words**; **N.H's earlier output is N.H's earlier output and is
not fact merely because N.H said it**; **speaker identity is carried
from the source and not guessed later**; **the provenance label does

Engine**. The exact final label value and schema format are
