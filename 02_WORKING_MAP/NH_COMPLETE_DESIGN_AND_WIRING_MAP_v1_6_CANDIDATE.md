## I.1 — Foundational laws (every component inherits these)

- **L1 — Never decide facts / never close the book (§0).** Every conclusion is a non-decisive accreting layer. Ness affirms off-board, not as a step in the machine.
- **L2 — DUMB vs SMART + the membrane (§0A).** DUMB moves/holds data and may state directly-verifiable machine-state/provenance facts; SMART reads the person and may never silently promote interpretation into fact. The membrane is the boundary; creation (SMART) never closes into memory (DUMB).
- **L3 — Full-transparency / living-record (§0B).** Every internal operation (retrieval, context choice, acceptance, rejection, omission, failure, use, non-use, state change, privacy check, clash, reread, connection) emits one append-only operational record: **one real operation → one log**; **no double evidence** (a log never makes its subject more certain or a second vote for itself); records are living memory (retrievable, connectable, recursively self-examinable); **active/cold** state with declared cooling rules N.H may only *propose* changing (Ness approves) and which reactivate by actual use or a valid new link, never similarity alone. A component without this recordkeeping is incomplete by design. *Every component below additionally states its own component-specific logging line; inheriting L3 in the abstract is not sufficient.*
- **L4 — No destruction (§0A/§0B/§7Q).** True destruction is prohibited everywhere. The only change a stored item undergoes is non-destructive visibility removal. Invalidated credentials are rendered permanently inert but preserved as history.
- **L5 — Two destinations / two files (§1).** Every eligible accepted input goes to a raw sealed **root** and a **reading** beside it that points back by id and never alters the raw.
- **L6 — Model confidence is metadata, not authority (§7G).** Acceptance depends on grounding, not on what the model claims about itself. Applies to all present/future models.
- **L7 — Relevance is not truth (§7R).** Relevance is purpose-scoped only; never evidence strength, causation, authority, or Ness's judgment.
- **L8 — Private-access-open-by-default; external restricted (§7Q).** For Ness's authenticated private use, material is eligible by default (sensitivity alone never withholds); external exposure, exports, tool use, notifications, secondary uses are restricted by default.

*Beyond L1–L8, a ninth cross-cutting contract binds every surface that talks to Ness or produces a file: the **C-2 Ness Interaction and Artifact-Delivery Contract** (§2/§2A — locked). It is specified as a component in §I.2 (Group G0) rather than restated here.*

### GROUP 0 — The meaning foundation (the Universal Filter and the Meaning Engine)

*These two entries map the conceptual and mechanism layer that the reading core (C-7G, C-7GA) implements per pass and that the engines (C-ENGINE-AB, C-ENGINE-C) build concretely. C-7G does **not** replace §7A or §7B; it is their per-pass realization.*

**C-7A — Universal Filter (§7A).**
Purpose/owner: the operating-rules layer of all meaning work — the principles every reading obeys. It is one continuous reader, not a pipeline of separate tools. Inputs→Outputs: material that has **passed the applicable §7Q capture-exclusion and internal-use-authorization rules for the current purpose**, **passed the relevant §7E / root-ingestion gate**, **resolved its required blockers**, and (**for TSC material**) **completed fingerprint authorization and promotion** — → a current best reading laid beside the raw, re-readable forever. **Capture-excluded, unauthorized, malformed, unresolved-speaker, or explicitly internal-use-blocked material does not enter ordinary reading.** But **restriction, hiding, redaction, or deletion from *visible output* does NOT automatically stop internal retrieval, analysis, connection, simulation, or influence** — restricted material **may still be read internally when the current authorized purpose permits it**, and **stopping internal influence requires Ness's separate explicit influence-removal instruction** (§7Q). The twelve-plus operating rules (exactly):
- **R0** reader/layer-er, not judge;
- **R0.5** never close the book;
- **R1** no *eligible* source type is exempt from the one filter (eligibility is the §7Q/§7E/blocker/TSC gate above — this is **not** a claim that every captured object is immediately read);
- **R2** sorts/reads, never closes "real";
- **R3** one continuous reader (segmentation and classification are one act — finding the boundary and naming the type are the same faculty, applied in a single pass);
- **R4** meaning from wide context (a statement cannot be classified from the words in front of it; the same words mean different things against the large accumulated frame);
- **R5** classification never locked (because context keeps accumulating, every reading is provisional and may be re-read and recolored later);
- **R5.5** the affirmation surface is a per-person STORY-layer (six optional parts; absence read ONLY under the §0A absence guard; CLASH surfaced not resolved; N.H is not a teller — its view is a weightless NOTE);
- **R6** memory only ADDS (governs HISTORY; the Computed View decides current use; new context adds a new layer beside the old reading, never overwrites it);
- **R7** the membrane (the DUMB/SMART boundary);
- **R8** associative bridging;
- **R9** sort by meaning-type, with **mode as a separate peer web** (not a master key);
- **R10** maximal-but-bounded;
- **R11** Ness steers and affirms **off-board** (sovereignty is standing authority to steer and override a record that never stops growing and never erases itself — not a moment of manual approval);
- **R12** honesty about what this is.
The keystone (settled): there are not two tools (extractor + classifier); there is one continuous reader in a single pass; meaning is context-dependent at scale; therefore classification is never final; the resolution to the manual-sorting contradiction is that **memory only adds, never edits** — the original reading stays, the new reading joins it, nothing is overwritten, and Ness is not a clerk.
Stores/records: produces readings (C-READ) laid beside sealed roots (C-STORE); the accretion of layered re-readings is the HISTORY. Upstream: all front doors via §7E. Downstream: §7G (per-pass realization), §7F (context), §7H (re-reading), §7K (story-layer), §7M (current-use selection). Gates: §7Q before §7R on every operation; L6 binds (model confidence is metadata). Recovery: every reading is provisional and re-readable; nothing is destroyed (L4). Logging (§0B): records each reading laid down, each re-reading layer added (with the context that prompted it), and each surfaced gap, so the evolution of meaning is itself an inspectable record; records subject to §7Q access and authorization rules, and §25 identity/security authorization where applicable. Boundaries/must-never: never judge/close "real"; never lock a classification; never overwrite an earlier reading; never act as a teller (its view is a NOTE); never become the manual gate it dissolved. Exact relationship to other components: **C-7G** realizes §7A per pass (acceptance check + creation-aware mode) and does not replace it; **C-7F** supplies the wide context R4 requires; **C-7H** is the R5/R6 re-reading-adds mechanism made operational; **C-7K** is the R5.5 per-person story-layer; **C-7M** is the R6 "Computed View decides current use" surface; and in the live cycles, §7A is the principle layer beneath CY-A (post-root reading), CY-B (chat), and CY-F (reread). Open slots: none new (R0–R12 are settled); its realizations carry the open slots of C-7G/C-7F/C-7H/C-7K/C-7M. Design-complete: R0–R12, the one-continuous-reader keystone, wide-context dependence, never-locked classification, memory-only-adds, the per-person story-layer, the off-board sovereignty rule, and the exact relationships above are stated and consistent with their realizations.

**C-7B — Meaning Engine Web Chain, Log, Note, and Wonder Boundary (§7B).**
Purpose/owner: the mechanism of meaning — how a piece is actually read. A piece's meaning is **not** one flat tag; it lives in how dimensions relate in this specific piece. Inputs→Outputs: a piece (against its wide accumulated context) → a reading whose dimensions are what holds up across all the webs together. **The seven webs, run together as ONE pass (not one-after-another as separate engines):**
1. **INTENT** — what they were doing by saying it (speech-act / illocutionary; the five-to-eight categories state/claim, ask, promise/intend, express/feel, declare, report, wonder, imagine live HERE as one web, not the whole machine);
2. **DEIXIS** — anchor the pointing words to reality (person / temporal / spatial / discourse deixis; distance may be psychological, not physical);
3. **COMMON GROUND** — what is shared and assumed (the unspoken backdrop both parties already knew);
4. **IMPLICATURE** — what they meant beyond the literal words (Gricean; the four maxims Quality / Quantity / Relation / Manner; a flout = the tell for sarcasm / irony / non-literal meaning — how a true claim is distinguished from a sarcastic one before anything is stored);
5. **THEORY OF MIND** — model the mind behind it (read the piece against who said it and their cognitive/affective state);
6. **TIME / SEQUENCE** — where it sits in the flow (a message moves report→feel→dismiss→intend; the piece is one state in that chain);
7. **RE-READING** — run the whole chain again later when new context arrives, producing a NEW reading added as a layer, never an overwrite (this IS the accretion; the re-reading web never truly closes).
The list is deliberately open: new webs may be named as real life reveals dimensions the current set cannot hold, but each must be a REAL, grounded dimension, never decorative. The webs combine as separate lenses stacked into one camera — light passes through all together and one image comes out; richness is breadth across webs plus the relationships between what lights up, not depth-drilling into subcategories.
**Part 0 — the Pure Tape:** verbatim, append-only, outside memory; capture-exclusions and two protection levels apply (§0A, §0B). **Part 2.5 — the Story-Layer Web:** the affirmation/perspective dimension read as a per-person STORY-layer (the web **C-7K** operationalizes) — tellings recorded unaltered, organized by perspective/theme/time, with clash between tellings preserved, never resolved. **Part 2.6 — Person-Boxes:** references to a person are gathered into a stable identity anchor (the structure **C-7L** operationalizes) — a GATHER bounded by Ness's knowing, never a synthesized profile. **Part 4 — nightly research feeds MEMORY, never the mouth:** the nightly deepening / research path (**C-8 / CY-C**) lands its findings in memory as readings and never trains or alters the mouth. **Part 5 — hold-until-enough:** when a piece cannot yet be responsibly read, the engine may hold rather than force a reading. The **policy meaning of "enough"** — what counts as enough to read responsibly, and which kind of insufficiency should cause **holding** rather than producing an **`insufficient_context`** reading — is an evidence/meaning threshold **reserved to Ness → A29**. The **mechanics** (where the held piece sits, its lifecycle, the release event, idempotency, crash recovery, and the explicit distinctions from `insufficient_context`, §7E pre-ingest holding, and technical retry — none of which it may be equated with) → **B-HOLD**. No threshold is chosen here. **Part 6 — can't-fill → inform, don't ask (the exact sovereignty rule):** if after researching inward and outward a web genuinely cannot be filled, the engine does NOT silently auto-stamp it "unknown" and does NOT stop to ask Ness to decide; it INFORMS Ness by surfacing the specific gap by name, keeps running, and blocks nothing on Ness; when Ness later supplies the answer it lands as a NEW LAYER ("unknown on [date] → filled by Ness on [later date]"); "unknown" is an honest, valid, re-checkable state, never a failure or a permanent verdict. (Live exception: the catalog front door MAY ask at capture time.) **Part 7 — the Log:** read-only / look-don't-touch (Ness can SEE everything the engine did but cannot EDIT it — the accretion rule made visible, so the record cannot lie about its own past); seeing is not approving (a log to read, not a queue to action — "inform, don't ask" as a browsable surface, no clerk work); subject-tagged (every entry carries subject = X); permanent and findable (filter by subject = X weeks later and the whole evolving history is intact); clickable (subjects expand; entries open to show the piece, its webs, its unknown-flags, and its layers over time). The Log is three things at once: transparency, the inward mirror, and a permanent subject-indexed archive. **Part 7.5 — the NOTE / the WHY:** N.H's own view sits inside SMART as a weightless NOTE; the "why" behind a connection is shown by pointers, never stored as an asserted fact (no `reason` field). **Part 6.5 — the Wonder / simulation boundary (settled portion only):** simulation scratch-space output cannot enter memory directly; the larger Wonder mechanism remains undesigned and is gated on A17.
Stores/records: the Pure Tape (verbatim append-only); readings carrying the seven-web content; the Log (read-only, subject-tagged). Upstream: front doors / Pure Tape. Downstream: §7G (per-pass realization of the chain + acceptance check), §7H (the re-reading web operationalized), §7K (the Story-Layer Web, Part 2.5), §7L (Person-Boxes, Part 2.6), C-8 / CY-C (nightly research feeds memory, Part 4), and C-ENGINE-C (the engine layer that fills the story-layer). Gates: §7Q exclusions on the Pure Tape; §7Q before §7R on derived operations; L6 binds. Recovery: re-reading adds layers; nothing overwritten; "unknown" re-checkable. Logging (§0B): the Log itself is this component's living-record surface — every nightly pass, every web fired, every unknown-flag, and every later-filled layer is recorded read-only and subject-tagged; records subject to §7Q access and authorization rules, and §25 identity/security authorization where applicable. Boundaries/must-never: never run the webs as separate sequential engines; never collapse meaning to the single intent tag; never stop-and-ask when a web cannot be filled (inform instead); never let the Log become editable or a queue; never store a "why" as an asserted fact; never let Wonder output cross into memory directly. Connections required by this entry: **C-7G** (the chain + Part-6 rule realized per pass), **C-7H** (the re-reading web), **C-ENGINE-C** (fills the story-layer the chain exposes), **CY-A** (the post-root pass runs the chain), **CY-B** (the live chat pass runs the chain), and **CY-J** (nightly deepening surfaces through the Log; Wonder stays scratch-space, gated A17). Open slots: the larger Wonder mechanism → A17; the story-layer object-identity seam where telling-level links are involved → A2; story-bearing gold for the engine layer → A1. Design-complete: the seven webs, one-pass combination, the exact inform-don't-ask rule, the read-only subject-tagged clickable Log, the NOTE/WHY position inside SMART, the Pure-Tape boundary, and the settled Wonder boundary are all stated, with the larger Wonder mechanism left to A17.

### GROUP F — Cross-cutting control

**C-7Q — Privacy, Deletion, Sensitive-data (§7Q).**
Purpose/owner: the gate that runs *before* relevance on every operation and *as the first sequential factor* before output. **Two authorization kinds:** internal-use authorization (for internal operations) and visible-output authorization, which has two parts in sequence — **Layer 1 pre-retrieval visible-output eligibility** (limits which material may become a candidate, before retrieval) and **Layer 2 pre-output privacy review** (after authorized material is retrieved and an output is formed, run as part of the §7Q privacy gate). Deletion: dependency+location search across all derivatives/indexes/embeddings/caches/exports/backups + indirect traces; five outcomes (`protected_complete` / `protected_with_declared_limits` / `incomplete` / `blocked` / `failed`); history record (not a tombstone; content preserved). Records: every privacy decision (operation, purpose, material, rule, authorization basis, eligibility decision, output-review result, withholding). Upstream/Downstream: every component routes its §7Q authorization through LMAC. **Output-gate sequence (settled):** §7Q is the **first** sequential factor (its pre-retrieval eligibility limits eligible material, and its pre-output review runs on the formed output); the **SACL final access/PBR gate is the second** factor; only then delivery. Gates: L8 (private-open-by-default; external-restricted); influence-removal is a **separate explicit instruction** with its own scope/record — hiding/restriction/deletion-from-view never stop internal influence alone; capture-exclusion two-layer (non-negotiable core vs Ness-configured); third-party baseline (attribute speaker, never present inferred states as facts, no profiling).
