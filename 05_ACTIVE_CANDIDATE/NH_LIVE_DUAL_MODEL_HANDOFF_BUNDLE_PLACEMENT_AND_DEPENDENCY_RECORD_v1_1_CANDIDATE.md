## 1. PURPOSE AND PLAIN MEANING

**Purpose.** The dual-model handoff decision was approved as a
standalone concept before its bundle placement was written down
anywhere. Without a placement record, the decision floats: it could be
half-built early, wired before its dependencies exist, or silently
treated as already scheduled. This record fixes that by stating its
owners, its position, and its open work — so the decision waits in the
right place, whole.

**Plain meaning of the approved decision (recorded, not expanded):**
one heavier local model thinks deeper in the background and proposes
substance; one lighter local model is the single voice Ness actually
talks to; N.H itself validates what both produce before anything is
spoken. Ness always talks to one voice. The heavy model never speaks;
the light model never invents the deep conclusion; N.H never lets
either become authority.

   expresses validated substance. (Unchosen; the single visible

borrowed frozen commodity; model confidence is metadata, never
authority; growth lands in memory, never the mouth; a model-provider
refusal is a mouth limitation, never an N.H privacy rule; DUMB does
not interpret and SMART never silently promotes interpretation into
fact.
