# 0. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

**What this package is.** It records Ness's Bundle-1 Story-Layer firmness
decision: firmness is recorded with **clear qualitative labels plus a
mandatory evidence basis — not numeric scoring and not fake precision.**
It fills exactly the slot Master §7K leaves open (scale form, labels) and
nothing more.

**What firmness means (required meaning, preserved):** firmness is **how
strongly the perspective owner appears to hold the stance.** It does not
mean the stance is true. It is separate from confidence, grounding, truth,
relevance, model confidence, and source reliability.

**What this package does not do:** it does not design the full Story Layer
schema; it does not decide theme schema, theme confirmation, similarity
grouping, or retrieval influence; it does not decide A31, A25, B9 values,
B10, B16, or B-CYCLE wiring; it chooses no storage, runtime, model, UI, or
implementation detail; it reopens nothing accepted (A2, B24, B-HOLD, B11,
B16, B9, B10, A29); it integrates nothing and adopts nothing.

# 1. NORMALIZED DECISION RECORD — NESS'S OPTION B

**Ness-approved decision (carried in the task instruction; preserved):**
Use **Option B**. N.H records firmness with careful qualitative labels and
a mandatory evidence basis. The allowed firmness labels for this package
are:

- `low_firmness`
- `moderate_firmness`
- `high_firmness`
- `mixed_firmness`
- `uncertain_firmness`
- `omitted_no_support`

Every firmness value must record **why** it was assigned, using
source-grounded evidence such as: direct self-report of certainty or doubt;
explicit certainty words; hedging; repetition; emphasis; consistency within
the root; direct statement of commitment or doubt; reported speech;
uncertain attribution; conflicting signals.

**Normalized statements (no new meaning added):**
1. The firmness scale is **qualitative labels only** — the six labels
   above. No numeric score, no percentage, no weight, no hidden scoring
   math exists anywhere in firmness.
2. Every **non-omitted** firmness value carries a recorded,
   source-grounded **evidence basis**.
3. Firmness is never forced: weak or absent evidence produces the honest
   lesser record (`uncertain_firmness`, `mixed_firmness`, or
   `omitted_no_support`), never an invented level.
4. Firmness is a bounded interpretation about how a stance **appears to be
   held** — never truth, never confidence, never direct access to anyone's
   internal state.

# 2. THE SIX LABELS — WHEN EACH IS USED

All criteria below are **qualitative judgments grounded in the source
material's observable signals**, under the settled evidential-strength
hierarchy (§3). No counting rule, signal quota, or threshold arithmetic is
created; "stronger/weaker" below is the Master's qualitative hierarchy,
never a score.

- **`high_firmness`** — the source shows the perspective owner holding the
  stance strongly: direct self-report of certainty or commitment, explicit
  certainty words, or strong consistent supporting signals (emphasis,
  consistency within the root) pointing the same way, with no conflicting
  signal of comparable evidential weight.
- **`moderate_firmness`** — the source supports that the stance is held
  with ordinary, plain commitment: the stance is asserted and maintained,
  but without strong certainty markers, or with mild qualification that
  does not amount to doubt.
- **`low_firmness`** — the source shows the stance held weakly or
  tentatively: hedging, tentative wording, expressed reservations, or a
  direct statement of doubt about the stance the owner nonetheless holds.
- **`mixed_firmness`** — an **active value, not a fallback**: the source
  carries genuinely conflicting firmness signals of comparable evidential
  weight about the same stance (for example, explicit certainty words
  together with real hedging), and the conflict cannot honestly be
  resolved to a single level. The conflict is recorded as the finding;
  it is never force-resolved.
- **`uncertain_firmness`** — firmness-relevant signals exist, so the
  question honestly arises, but they are too ambiguous, too weak, or too
  attribution-degraded (reported speech, uncertain attribution) to
  responsibly name a level or a genuine two-sided conflict. The honest
  finding is that the owner's firmness cannot be determined from these
  signals.
- **`omitted_no_support`** — the root does not support a firmness judgment
  at all: no usable firmness-relevant signal exists. Nothing is asserted;
  no placeholder is invented.

**Never-force rule across the boundaries:** when the engine cannot honestly
distinguish between two of these outcomes, the **less-claiming record is
the correct one** — a level is never preferred over `mixed_firmness` or
`uncertain_firmness` where the conflict or uncertainty is real, and no
value is preferred over `omitted_no_support` where even the presence of
usable signals is not supported. This is the Master's
never-forced-resolution rule applied, not a new tie-break mechanism.

**Representation consistency (accepted A2 preserved, unchanged):**
`omitted_no_support` is the **policy name for the no-support outcome**. In
the accepted A2 first-class telling card — and in the built v1 embedded
`story_layer` entry — that outcome is expressed exactly as those contracts
already fix it: the `firmness` field is **honestly absent** (omitted; never
`"unknown"`; never a filled placeholder). Nothing here writes a literal
token into a field whose accepted contract expresses omission by absence.
Whether any **other** surface (annotation, display, a future schema) writes
the explicit token `omitted_no_support` is a representation question for
that surface's owning schema work — not decided here.

# 3. THE MANDATORY EVIDENCE BASIS

- **Required for every non-omitted value.** Each `low_firmness`,
  `moderate_firmness`, `high_firmness`, `mixed_firmness`, and
  `uncertain_firmness` assignment records its evidence basis — the
  observable, source-grounded signals it rests on. `omitted_no_support`
  asserts nothing and requires no basis.
- **Source-grounded only.** The basis cites signals actually present in
  the source material, of the approved kinds: direct self-report of
  certainty or doubt; explicit certainty words; hedging; repetition;
  emphasis; consistency within the root; direct statement of commitment or
  doubt; reported speech; uncertain attribution; conflicting signals. The
  governing requirement is source-groundedness — a basis is never tone,
  style, or wording **alone** treated as direct access to internal state.
- **Qualitative strength hierarchy (preserved from Master §7K; never
  scored):** direct self-report of certainty or doubt is **stronger**
  evidence than tone, wording style, or repetition alone; **reported
  speech or uncertain attribution lowers evidential strength.** These
  orderings inform the label judgment qualitatively; they are never
  converted into numbers, weights, or arithmetic.
- **Record home (accepted A2 preserved):** where the first-class telling
  card exists, the basis lives in the accepted `firmness_evidence_basis`
  field beside `firmness`; a legacy embedded `firmness` lacking a recorded
  basis follows A2 §4A unchanged — the basis is **never invented** to
  satisfy the contract.
- **The basis is transparency, not a second vote.** Recording the basis
  adds inspectability; it never adds certainty, and repeated citation of
  the same signals never strengthens the value (§0B no-double-evidence).

---

# 4. SEPARATIONS PRESERVED — WHAT FIRMNESS IS NOT

- **Not truth.** A `high_firmness` telling asserts nothing about whether
  the stance is correct. Firmness is never a truth score.
- **Not confidence.** The reading's two-slot `confidence`
  (`interpretation_confidence`, `source_reliability`) is reading-level
  metadata owned by the reading (accepted A2 one-authority rule); firmness
  is telling-level and lives on the telling. Neither is derived from,
  copied into, or blended with the other.
- **Not grounding.** How much support a proposal needs to pass the
  acceptance check is **A31 — open**; firmness never substitutes for it.
- **Not a reading outcome.** Firmness labels are values **inside completed
  readings' tellings**. They never substitute for the §7G honest
  `insufficient_context` reading or an A29 hold, and no firmness value
  triggers, blocks, or releases either.
- **Not relevance, not model confidence, not source reliability.** Each
  remains its own settled dimension with its own owner; none collapses
  into firmness.
- **Not direct inner-state access.** N.H must never present firmness as
  direct access to a person's internal state — it is an inference from
  observable signals, and it says so.

---

# 5. PROVISIONAL, REVISABLE, HONEST

- **Provisional and revisable, always.** A firmness value is a dated,
  bounded interpretation. Revision happens only the settled way: a new
  reading/telling beside the old through the §7H reread lifecycle and the
  A2 new-id-per-new-telling rule — **never** by editing, overwriting, or
  deleting the earlier record.
- **Conflicting signals never force-resolve** — they produce
  `mixed_firmness`, `uncertain_firmness`, or omission (§2), exactly as the
  Master fixes.
- **Repetition is not confirmation.** Repetition within the root is one
  observable signal about how the stance is expressed there; repetition
  **across** records never raises firmness by accumulation, never confirms
  the stance, and never becomes multiple votes for the same underlying
  evidence (§0B no-double-evidence).
- **No fake precision, no hidden scoring.** No numeric firmness value,
  percentage, weight, ranking function, or threshold arithmetic exists —
  visibly or internally. Any future quantitative proposal would be a new
  Ness policy decision through a new versioned candidate, not a mechanical
  refinement.

# 6. STORY-LAYER SOUL RULES PRESERVED (BINDING CONTEXT, UNCHANGED)

- Story-Layer tellings remain **bounded interpretations, never facts**.
- The Story Layer **never merges conflicting tellings** into one
  synthesized narrative and **never decides whose telling is correct** —
  conflict is surfaced, preserved, and navigable.
- A telling's firmness label never promotes the telling toward fact,
  never resolves a clash, and never selects a winner between tellings.
- Engine-proposed themes remain `proposed`; only Ness confirms; nothing
  here touches the theme system.
